Why I made PixlPaint

I built PixlPaint for my own kids. I wanted them to have a bit of calm, focused screen time that felt like theirs, without feeds, notifications, or ads nudging them toward social media.

Coloring is great for that. Filling in one pixel at a time is easy to get absorbed in. It's relaxing, it helps with focus, and it's good for fine motor skills too.

That's the idea. A quiet spot to slow down and make something.

Adam, dad and maker of PixlPaint
