PixlPaint — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026 · Provider: AJara · Contact: pixlpaintapp@gmail.com
In short: PixlPaint does not collect, store, or share any personal information. No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking. Apart from processing optional purchases through Apple (see Purchases), the app works entirely on your device, and nothing you do in PixlPaint is sent to us.

Information we collect

None. PixlPaint does not collect personal information of any kind. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, no email collection, and no third-party advertising or analytics.

Information stored on your device

PixlPaint saves your progress locally on your device only — for example, which pictures you've colored, your stars and coins, your power-up inventory, and pictures you create. This data never leaves the device, is not transmitted to us, and is removed if you delete the app.

Camera and photo access

If you choose "Make from a Photo," PixlPaint asks permission to use your photo library or camera only so it can turn a picture you pick into a coloring page on your device. The image is processed locally into colorable pixels. PixlPaint does not upload, store, transmit, or share your photos, and does not access your library or camera unless you tap that feature.

Children's privacy

PixlPaint is designed for children and complies with children's privacy laws (including COPPA and GDPR-K). Because PixlPaint collects no personal information and contains no third-party tracking or advertising, there is no personal data of children (or anyone) for us to collect, use, or disclose.

Purchases

PixlPaint offers optional one-time picture packs you can buy. All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store — PixlPaint never sees your name, payment details, or other personal information. To unlock what you've bought (and to power Restore Purchases), the app uses RevenueCat, a purchase-management service, which receives only a randomly generated, anonymous purchase identifier and the fact that a pack was bought. This is not linked to your identity, is not used for tracking or advertising, and contains no personal information about you or your child. Every purchase requires a grown-up to pass a parental check first.

Third parties

PixlPaint uses no third-party advertising, analytics, or data-collection services. The only third parties involved are Apple (which processes payments) and RevenueCat (which manages and restores optional purchases); they receive no personal information about you or your child — see Purchases.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
