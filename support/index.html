Frequently asked

How do I restore purchases I already made?

Open More Pictures (the cart icon on the home screen) and tap Restore Purchases at the bottom. Your packs unlock on any device signed in to the same Apple ID.

Does PixlPaint work without internet?

Yes — PixlPaint works fully offline. The only thing that needs a connection is making or restoring a purchase, which the App Store handles.

Are there ads or tracking?

No. No ads, no analytics, no third-party tracking, and no accounts or sign-in. Everything your child does stays on the device.

How do the picture packs work?

Packs (like Dino World and Mythic & Magic) are optional one-time purchases, yours to keep. A grown-up confirms every purchase behind a parental check (a quick math question).

Can coins be bought with money?

No. In-game coins are earned by playing and are used for fun power-ups. Money only ever unlocks picture packs — never coins.

How do I turn a photo into a coloring page?

Tap Make from a Photo and pick an image. PixlPaint turns it into colorable pixels right on the device — the photo is never uploaded or shared.

How do I delete my data?

All data is stored only on your device. Deleting the app removes everything.
